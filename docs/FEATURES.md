## Introduction

**AI WebScraper by CushLabs** is an AI-powered website crawling and analysis platform. You give it a URL; it crawls the site page by page, captures the real content and structure, detects the technical and SEO problems that quietly cost you traffic, and then runs that data through AI to produce a plain-English, prioritized action plan — complete with copy-paste fixes.

Most "SEO tools" dump a wall of metrics on you and leave the thinking as your problem. AI WebScraper is built to do the opposite: it behaves less like a linter and **more like a strategic consultant**, telling you _what's wrong, why it matters, and exactly what to change first._

### 2. Technical SEO & site-health detection

Automated detection of the **9 highest-signal issues**, each severity-rated (critical → medium):

- 🔴 Broken internal links · 🟠 Broken images · 🟠 Oversized (slow) pages
- 🟠 Missing image alt text · 🟠 Duplicate title tags
- 🟡 Duplicate meta descriptions · 🟡 Missing H1 headings · 🟡 Thin content · 🟡 Orphan pages

**Benefit:** the problems that actually move rankings and accessibility get surfaced and prioritized automatically — no spreadsheet archaeology.
